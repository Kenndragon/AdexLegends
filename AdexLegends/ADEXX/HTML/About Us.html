GAME MODES
ARENA
Arena is the place for you and your teammates to fight other teams in 3 vs 3. At the start of the round, purchase weapons and other items in shop.The first team to win three rounds with a two round margin is victorious. If the score is 4-4, then the match goes to sudden death.
BATTLE ROYALE
In battle royale, there are 60 players who will battle until there is only one squad left. There will be a border that will keep shrinking. You will take a lot of damage if you're outside the border. Find better equipment by exploring the map and kill other players. Stay alive until the end to win.
OVERVIEW
STRATEGIC GAMEPLAY
Both in arena mode and in battle royale, you can't win alone. You need to work together and create a good strategy. Create new tactics and try unique combo in order to stay alive and win.
INNOVATIVE COMBAT
Master any type of weapons and equipments. They have their own style and still so much to discover. There will be updates about it every month, so you will never get bored.
HUGE AND GORGEOUS UNIVERSE
The place where Adex Legends take places. There are many hidden things inside which you should discover it. Lastly, don't worry because every season the map will keep changing.
