ANCIENT
Newest addition in Adex Legends map. Ancient has lots of trees and ruins for you to take cover and to kill people.
TROPICAL
Tropical is the biggest arena map in Adex Legends. This place doesn't have many object for you to take cover. So always stay alert and watch your back.
CITY
Welcome to the City, the smallest arena map, where there are lots of buildings and cars. Use them to hide and take cover from the enemy.
AIRPORT
Welcome to Adex Legend's Airport. This map is enormous. Take your time to explore this place. Don't forget to check the planes, there might be something on it.

KINGDOM
Adex Legends most popular map. There are magnificent castles everywhere you go. Make sure to check every rooms there, many treasures await for you.
WINTER
Adex Legends' coldest map. This map is covered by snow and doesn't have many places to hide which makes it easy to see other people and vice versa.
INFERNO
Inferno, the smallest battle royale map. This map is surrounded by lava. Be careful not to fall into the lava or you will suffer serious damage.
